Services

We offer 4 types of cleaning services using products of your choice & or eco-friendly products, we offer a hybrid clean and a few extras if required. We come with everything in tow ready to add some sparkle to any space. Our team is a unique bunch that loves, lives, and dreams of cleaning, it's important to us to add that extra layer of detail and fuss. All of our cleaners are highly skilled at what they do as we've searched high and low to make sure we provide the best. Our staff have NDIS screening checks, Police checks, and WWC cards, we ensure all checks are up to date and reviewed regularly.
