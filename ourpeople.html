About Us



When it comes to finding support and services for yourself or a loved one it can be hard to know where to start, that's why we have created Akura Care. Our aim is to support others within our community and be there to help them live a more fulfilling and rewarding life. Akura care caters to the community by offering High-Quality services which makes the day to day living and life in general a little bit easier.

At Akura Care we offer services such as.

- Personal Support Work (Disability) - Companionship for our Elderly - Baby Sitting - Cleaning - Gardening and Maintenance.

Our team specialises in these areas, with the right qualifications along with checks and insurances in place for their fields. We have searched high and low to find the right people for you, we are passionate about our areas of expertise and hope that by raising the standards, others will follow in our place.
